About

 ABOUT US
Al Maksab Food & Beverage Trading Company L.L.C., established in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. Al Maksab is the exclusive agent and distributor of several high-quality products in Fresh Fruits, Vegetables, Dates, Grains, Cereals, Legumes, Flour, Dried Fruits Vegetables, Canned Beverages, Fresh chilled Meat items and Seafood. We operate mainly and directly in the Gulf Council Countries (GCC).  We are a wholesaler and Retailer in the market and We import Export high quality brands from several parts of the world.
